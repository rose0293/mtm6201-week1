Abhinav Rosemary Raj

Abhinav Rosemary Raj is a design student currently pursuing a diploma in Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College Ottawa, Canada. Born on May 1, 2003, in Kerala, the so-called God's own country in India, known for its lush green landscapes and backwaters.

From a young age, he got his passion for creativity and art from his brother, and as he grew up, he became increasingly interested in design and started dreaming a career as a freelance designer. He decided to put the Interactive Media Design program as a stepping stone for his career, by working on his skills in this field, he is looking forward to leaving a mark in this industry to inspire others who loves design or who are looking to be a designer. He loves to read about history and self improvement books and he sees life challenges as personal development to be a better person.
